# Project Overview:
This project delivers a comprehensive analysis of the Olist e-commerce dataset, exploring sales performance, customer behavior, payment trends, and delivery efficiency. Through data cleaning, transformation, and visualization, it uncovers key insights that influence business decision-making. An interactive dashboard further enhances the understanding of marketplace operations.

# Dataset Used:
This project uses the Olist E-commerce Public Dataset, containing 100K+ orders with customer demographics, product categories, payment methods, delivery performance, and customer review data.

## Required Key Performance Indicators (KPI's):
- Total Sales – Overall revenue generated across all orders.
- Total Orders – Total number of orders placed in the dataset.
- Total Customers – Count of unique customers who made purchases.
- Total Sellers – Number of active sellers in the marketplace.
- Average Order Value (AOV) – Average revenue per order.
- Average Unit Price – Average selling price of individual items.
- Total Quantity Sold – Total number of product units sold.
- Average Review Score – Mean customer review rating across all orders.
- Average Delivery Time – Average time taken from purchase to delivery.
- Payment Method Distribution – Share of each payment type (credit, boleto, debit, etc.).
- Weekday vs Weekend Orders – Order volume comparison based on purchase day.
- Top-Performing Cities – Cities contributing the highest sales and revenue.
- Category-wise Sales – Revenue and quantity breakdown by product category.
- Delivery Time vs Review Score – Customer satisfaction impact based on shipping duration.
- Monthly/Quarterly Sales Trends – Revenue patterns across time periods.

# Project Process:
- Data Collection & Understanding – Imported the Olist SQL dataset and analyzed table structures, relationships, and data definitions.
- Data Cleaning & Transformation (ETL) – Handled missing values, standardized formats, converted timestamps, and prepared processed datasets for analysis.
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) – Examined sales trends, customer patterns, product performance, and delivery timelines using statistical summaries and visualizations.
- KPI Development – Identified and computed key performance indicators essential for evaluating marketplace performance.
- Dashboard Creation – Designed and built interactive visual dashboards to communicate insights clearly and effectively.
- Insights & Reporting – Generated actionable insights and summarized findings to support data-driven business decisions.
